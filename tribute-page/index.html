Timeline

  • 1842 - Born on January 11th at the Astor House in New York City.

  • 1843 - Brother Henry James is born on April 15th.

  • 1864 - Begins studies at Harvard Medical School.

  • 1869 - Earns his medical degree, but never goes on to practice medicine.

  • 1890 - Publishes the two-volume work The Principles of Psychology.

  • 1897 - Publishes The Will to Believe.

  • 1902 - Publishes The Varieties of Religious Experience.

  • 1907 - Publishes Pragmatism: A New Name for Some Old Ways of Thinking.

  • 1912 - Publishes Essays in Radical Empiricism.

  • 1910 - Dies of congestive heart failure in Chocorua, New Hampshire.
